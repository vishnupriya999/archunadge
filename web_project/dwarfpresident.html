Dwarf President Canna

Best in full sun. Regular water during growth and bloom. Can grow 2.5-3 feet tall. Has large lance-shaped leave. Leaf color can be green or bronzy red. Red flowers. Perfect in borders, near poolside, in large pots, tubs on terrace or on the patio.


Colors: Red/Green

Kind:Evergreen

Form:Shrubs

Characteristics:Desert Conditions
