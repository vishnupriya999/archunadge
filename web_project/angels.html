Angel's Trumpet, Pink Delight

Plant can live in sun or shade. Needs to be watered regularly. Grows to 15 feet tall and 15 feet wide. Flowers are 15 inches big and are a peach/ apricot color. Flowers hang straight from branches and covers the plant when in bloom. Blossoms are most fragrant in the morning and in the evening.


Colors: Green,Pink

Kind: Evergreen to Semi-Evergreen

Form:Tree

Characteristics:Pink Flowering
