Dwarf Heavenly Bamboo

Some shade in hottest climates. Little to regular water. 4-5 feet tall and 3 feet wide. Lacy looking. Narrow leaflets. Foliage is green and turns red, purple and orange. White flowers.


Colors: Green, Red, Orange

Kind:Evergreen or Semi-Evergreen

Form:Hedge

Characteristics:Fall Colors, Seacoast Conditions, Drought Resistant, Cold Hardy, Shade Tolerant, White Flowering
