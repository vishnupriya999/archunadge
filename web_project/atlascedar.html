Atlas Glauca Cedar, Blue Atlas Cedar

Best in full sun. Requires moderate watering. Plant grows moderately to 60 feet or taller. Tips of branches should be cut back regularly to avoid getting too long or heavy. Needles grow to less than
1 inch long and are bluish green in color.


Colors: Blue/Green

Kind:Evergreen

Form:Tree

Characteristics:Fire Retardant, Cold Hardy.
