Avondale Chinese Redbud

Best in full sun or light shade. Requires moderate to regular watering. Plant grows 10-12 feet tall and 10 feet wide. Flower clusters are 3-5 inches long and are deep rose to deep purple in color. Leaves are glossy and green in color.


Colors: Green,Purple

Kind: Deciduous

Form:Tree

Characteristics: Shade Tolerant, Blue/Purple Flowering
