Mexican Evening Primrose

Full sun or partial shade. Little to moderate water. 1 foot tall and 3 feet wide or more. 1-3 inch long, medium-green rosettes. 2 inch, fragrant pink-white flowers bloom spring-early summer


Colors: White/Green,Pink

Kind:Evergreen

Form: Perennial

Characteristics:Fire Retardant, Native Plants, Seacoast Conditions, Desert Conditions, Drought Resistant, Cold Hardy
