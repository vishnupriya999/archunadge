Hybrid Sweet Bay

Full sun or partial shade. Moderate water. 12-40
feet tall and just as wide. Oval, 2-4 inch long, leathery leaves. Flowers are small, yellow and grow in clusters. 0.5-1 inch black or dark purple fruit.


Colors: Green/Yellow

Kind:Evergreen

Form:Tree,Hedge

Characteristics:Fire Retardant, Seacoast Conditions, Desert Conditions, Shade Tolerant, Yellow/Orange Flowering
