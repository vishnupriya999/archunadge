Pink Powder Puff

Best in full sun. Requires regular watering. Flowers have long, silky stamens and blossoms have the appearance of a powder puff or a feather duster. blooms during the spring months. Has ferny foliage. Leaflets are a glossy copper and turn dark green. Grows quickly to about 10 feet high and wide or more


Colors: Pink/Green

Kind:Evergreen

Form:Shrubs

Characteristics:Deer Resistant, Pink Flowering
